Hot Springs Café • Since 1957


Tradition since 1957

Rich Taste in Coffees, Beverages and Bakery

The cafe was founded in Trivandrum by Mr. Nallas Thomson in 1957 and grew steadily from Mysore to Bangalore as demand for its warm hospitality increased.

Today, Hot Springs Cafe and Bakery is known for premium coffee, elegant desserts, and a French-cafe spirit served across 13+ cities in India, 2 in France, and 25+ in the USA.
